The top left subwindow displays the structure of the repository, including the head, the master branch, and the commits.

The top center subwindow shows information about the author of each commit.

The top right subwindow gives the date and time of each commit.

The bottom left subwindow displays specific information about the commit such as the author, the parent and child commits, the timestamp, the ChangeLog, and changes in diff format.

The bottom right subwindow shows the files that were changed by the commit.
